NHL Schedule

Show a team’s next matchup or most recent game.

Pick an NHL club, optionally add your own title, and the plugin will display either today’s game, the next scheduled matchup, or the most recent finished game when that team is inactive.

Matchup settings
Choose the team and optional title shown on the display.

Leave blank to use the generated matchup title automatically.

If the team has no upcoming game, the plugin falls back to that team’s most recent one.
